Our Journey

Our Story

Sunflower Trip was born out of a love for discovering new cultures and sharing unique experiences. From humble beginnings in Kolkata, we have grown into a trusted name in travel, offering curated trips to breathtaking destinations across the globe.

What began as a passion project among travel enthusiasts has evolved into a full-service travel agency dedicated to creating memories that last a lifetime. Our journey through the years has been about building trust, understanding travelers' needs, and delivering experiences that go beyond expectations.

Who We Are

About Sunflower Trip

Sunflower Trip Private Limited is one of India’s leading leisure and holiday travel companies. We offer thoughtfully crafted domestic and international travel experiences tailored to our clients’ dreams. Whether you seek soulful getaways, thrilling adventures, or luxurious escapes, our Kolkata-based team ensures every detail is personalized with care.

Our logo, inspired by the ever-optimistic sunflower, symbolizes our mission: to face the world with warmth and positivity. Like the sunflower, we guide you toward radiant and unforgettable journeys.

For the independent traveler, we offer flexible assistance—safety, suggestions, and backup services—while allowing you to travel freely and confidently.

Health & Safety

  • Face Masks: Required in public areas to ensure safe interactions.
  • Certified Food: All meals are FSSAI-certified to meet hygiene standards.
  • Sanitized Rooms: Every accommodation is cleaned thoroughly before your arrival.
  • Optional Housekeeping: Housekeeping can be adjusted based on your comfort to reduce contact.

Your safety is our top priority. We adhere to CDC and local authority guidelines to ensure your journey is both joyful and secure.
